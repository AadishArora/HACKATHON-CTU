About HealthBox

HealthBox is an innovative medical diagnostic system that leverages the power of artificial intelligence and natural language processing.

Our system uses advanced machine learning algorithms trained on extensive medical datasets to provide accurate diagnoses based on user-reported symptoms. The AI engine continuously learns and improves from each interaction.

Note: While HealthBox provides valuable health insights, it is not a replacement for professional medical care. Always consult with a healthcare provider for serious or persistent health concerns.
